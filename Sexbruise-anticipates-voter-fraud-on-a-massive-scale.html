CHARLESTON, SC - The Highly controversial band Sexbruise? said in an interview with Rolling Stone this week that they believe Charleston City Paper Music Awards are most likely rigged, and that the only way to know for sure will be if they lose to another band.

Here is the band’s official statement on the matter:

"The reasons we should win Best Electronic/Experimental Act are obvious: 1) We have the most likes by far 2)We throw the most snacks 3) All our likes are real 4) If we don’t win, it is because millions voted illegally. 5) This message is brought to you by Goodies Headache Powder and approved by Sexbruise?"

The bands comments have drawn criticism from almost no one, as most people are just going on living their lives as usual and seem unaware that there is even a contest going on.
